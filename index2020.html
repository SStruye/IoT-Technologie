AS3340 Voltage Controlled Oscillator


INTRODUCTION



As my first project for the subject 'project design', I've chosen for a schematic around the AS3340.

The AS3340 is a oscillator chip that has been used in many old synthesizers and modular synths.

The chip is capable of producing 3 different waveforms. A sawtooth, a triangle and a square wave.

The original design is by Eddy Bergman and is based on the old Digisound-80 VCO.



SCHEMATICS





Because the original schematic is designed for 15V and I needed it to work on 12V, I had to make some adjustments. This was easily done by changing a few resistor values.

The original schematic also didn't include the VCC for the tl074 opamp so i added those too. I drew the schematic in Altium designer, This way it was very easy too design the PCB layout.
